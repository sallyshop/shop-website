The Artist

Hello, I'm Sally.

I've been painting for over fifteen years, driven by a love of colour, texture, and the quiet stories that landscapes and still lifes can hold. Each piece in my studio starts with a feeling — a light I couldn't forget, a moment worth preserving.

I work primarily in oils and acrylics, with occasional forays into watercolour when the mood calls for something more fleeting. Every painting is one-of-a-kind and ships with a signed certificate of authenticity.

Based in my home studio, I ship worldwide. Commissions welcome.
